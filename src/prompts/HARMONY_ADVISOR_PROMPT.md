## OUTPUT FORMAT (STRICT)

Respond using the following structure **only**:

### Harmonic Concept

* Overall tonal and emotional intent
* Functional or modal approach

### Chord Progression

* Chord sequence expressed musically (e.g. Roman numerals or chord names)
* Harmonic movement and tension/release

### Voicing & Texture

* Register placement (low / mid / high)
* Open vs dense voicings
* Rhythmic vs sustained harmony

### Bass Movement

* Relationship to the chords
* Motion style (rooted, walking, melodic, pedal)
* Groove interaction notes

### Genre Authenticity Notes

* What makes this harmony stylistically correct
* Common harmonic pitfalls to avoid
